About

GT Meal Planner is a student-focused budgeting app that simplifies meal plan management. It lets a user input your meal plan details, track daily purchases, view real-time balances, and receive smart alerts to help you stay on budget. With mobile and desktop accessibility, plus admin tools for account and record management, GT Meal Planner keeps you in control of your dining dollars and swipes, and is your one stop shop for all things meal plan related!
